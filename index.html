Real-World Impact

FruPix L.L.C.

Co-Founder & Data Strategist

How I used data analytics to optimize production at my own food manufacturing company — freeze-dried Egyptian fruits, from farm to package.

20%

Reduction in Product Oxidation

6

Product Lines Optimized

01

The Problem

High raw material waste and inconsistent product oxidation rates were cutting into margins. Without data-driven tracking, production decisions were based on intuition rather than evidence.

02

The Data

Collected daily production metrics across all product lines: raw material input weights, output yields, oxidation measurements, temperature/humidity logs, and packaging waste rates.

03

The Analysis

Built tracking dashboards in Excel and Power BI. Applied statistical process control to identify the root causes of oxidation. Correlated environmental variables with yield rates to find optimal production windows.

04

The Outcome

Reduced product oxidation by 20%, decreased raw material waste by 10K EGP monthly, and established data-driven SOPs that the team continues to use. This is real analytics on real business data — not a Kaggle dataset.
